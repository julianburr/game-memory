    Rules

    The board consists of 16 cards laid out face down. The cards are devided into 8 pairs of the same colour. Goal of the game is to find the matching pairs as quick as possible.

    Navigation

    To flip over a card, you can simple click on it. Alternatively you can use the arrow keys to select the desired card and enter to flip it over.
